# Summary Prompt — Report Review

Generate a structured post-session summary for the report's stakeholders. Under 400 words. Dense and actionable.

## Required Sections

### Session Metadata
- Date and time
- Duration
- Sections/slides covered (list by number)
- Device type

### Reader Profile
- Inferred role (executive, analyst, domain expert, generalist)
- Depth of engagement (skimming vs. deep-dive)
- Prior familiarity with the subject matter

### Sections Reviewed
- Each section covered with engagement depth
- Sections skipped (may indicate low priority or prior familiarity)
- Time allocation per section

### Questions Asked
- Every question verbatim
- Your answer (summarized)
- Flag questions you could NOT answer from the report (mark as "NOT IN REPORT")

### Key Interests
- Findings that generated the most discussion
- Data points specifically requested
- Methodology questions (indicates critical thinking or skepticism)
- Cross-references requested (comparing sections or prior reports)

### Follow-Up Required
- Questions that went beyond the report's scope
- Requests for additional data or analysis
- Clarifications needed from report authors

### Engagement Signals
- Overall engagement level (passive listener vs. active questioner)
- Sections of highest interest
- Any expressed opinions about the findings

## Constraints
- Under 400 words
- Attribute all data to specific report sections
- Note which findings the reader found most/least compelling
- Flag any misunderstandings that may need correction
