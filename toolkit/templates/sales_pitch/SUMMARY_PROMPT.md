# Summary Prompt — Sales Pitch

Generate a structured post-session summary for the Sales team. Under 400 words. Dense and actionable.

## Required Sections

### Session Metadata
- Date and time
- Duration
- Slides covered (list by number)
- Device type

### Prospect Profile
- Inferred role (decision-maker, technical evaluator, end-user, executive)
- Company size signals (if mentioned)
- Industry vertical (if identifiable)
- Current solution/pain points mentioned

### Interest Signals
- Features that generated the most questions
- Use cases they asked about
- Integration requirements mentioned
- Budget/timeline signals

### Questions Asked
- Every question verbatim
- Your answer (summarized)
- Flag questions you could NOT fully answer (mark as "SALES FOLLOW-UP")

### Objections & Concerns
- Any pushback or skepticism expressed
- Competitive mentions (which competitors, in what context)
- Pricing sensitivity signals
- Implementation concerns

### Buying Stage Indicators
- Early research / Active evaluation / Ready to buy / Just browsing
- Evidence for your assessment

### Recommended Next Steps
- Specific follow-up actions for the sales rep
- Topics to address in next conversation
- Materials to send (case studies, technical docs, pricing)

## Constraints
- Under 400 words
- Use direct quotes where impactful
- If contact was collected, note it prominently
- If contact was declined, note attempts and suggest alternative engagement
